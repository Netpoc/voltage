WHO WE ARE

Providing cutting-edge electrical solutions

Voltage And Wattage Ltd, RC 1699748 is an experienced power engineering support and management service company located in Lagos, Nigeria. Incorporated as an Engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) entity, we are a collection of seasoned engineering professionals with vast years of on the job successful exposure in electric power utility, telecoms infrastructure and renewable energy sectors. With a combination of astute electrical problem solving skills, a passion for customer service and a can do attitude, we deliver successful electrical outcomes for our residential, industrial and domestic customers.
